Game Plan
T1 — Chalice + Colorless Ramp
The opener is Ancient Tomb → Chalice of the Void@0 or @1. Chalice@0 blanks Mox, Bauble, Petal; @1 blanks Brainstorm, Ponder, Delver, DRC, Bolt. Against blue decks → Chalice@1. Against artifact decks → Chalice@0.
T2 — Reality Smasher / TKS
Eldrazi Temple + Tomb = 4 mana T2 → Reality Smasher (5/5 haste, trample, ward 3 cards) or Thought-Knot Seer (4/4 + strip a nonland). Both are uncounterable under Chalice@1 because they're colorless → CMC doesn't match any number Chalice named.
T3+ — Press the Advantage
Deploy additional threats: Endless One (X/X for X mana — scales), Skittering Invasion for blockers/tokens, Dismember (1/4 life) for removal. The clock is typically T5 for lethal. If opponent stabilizes, Emrakul as SB top-end.
