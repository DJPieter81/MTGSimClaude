In play_turn() and protagonist_turn(), all cheap spells get cmc raised to max(cmc, 3) before strategy dispatch. Every strategy automatically pays the Trinisphere tax. Also blocks FoW/FoN alternate costs. LED costs 3 under Trinisphere (artifact, CMC 0 ŌåÆ taxed to 3).

Single function handles both directions. Scans defender hand for counter tags, classifies threat, checks Trinisphere/Veil, then walks FoNŌåÆFoWŌåÆCSŌåÆFlusterŌåÆPyroŌåÆDaze. Hand-size gates: CS needs 4+ cards. Veil of Summer and Allosaurus Shepherd bypass entirely.
