Matchup Strategy Guide

vs Combo (Storm/Oops/Doomsday)
Mulligan aggressively for Force of Will. T1 Thoughtseize their combo piece or protection (Veil of Summer). Deploy clock ASAP — every turn they don't combo is a turn you deal damage. Hold Daze mana open T1-3.
vs Reanimator
FoW the Reanimate/Exhume, not the discard. Thoughtseize their Griselbrand/Archon. If they resolve a fatty, race with Murktide (flying ignores ground blockers). Surgical post-board is key.
vs Show and Tell
Counter Show and Tell itself (not the cantrips). Thoughtseize Emrakul or Omniscience. Deploy threats fast — Show needs time to assemble. FoN comes in from sideboard for free counters.
vs Mirror (Dimir variants)
Win the Bowmasters war — whoever resolves theirs first controls the draw step. Murktide is the trump card (flies over everything). Wasteland their Underground Sea to cut colors. Protect your threats with Daze.
vs DnT / Boros
FoW their Aether Vial T1 — this is the most important counter of the game. Push Thalia immediately. Fly over with Murktide (they have no flyers). Wasteland their Karakas/Port. Don't walk into Eidolon — sequence 1-mana spells first.
vs UWx Control
Resolve Murktide as early as possible — UWx has few answers to an 8/8 flyer. Wasteland their dual lands. Play around Terminus (don't overcommit to board). Thoughtseize their Mentor before it generates tokens.
vs Prison
FoW Chalice on 1 (blocks your entire 1-mana package). If Trinisphere resolves, you can't FoW for free anymore. Deploy creatures T1-2 before locks land. Abrupt Decay answers Chalice/Bridge post-board.
vs Painter
FoW the Painter's Servant (enables instant kill with Grindstone). Watch for Blood Moon — it turns your duals into Mountains. Push the Grindstone. Pyroblast from their side counters your blue spells.
