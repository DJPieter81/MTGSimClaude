Matchup Strategy Guide

D vs Combo (Storm / Oops / Doomsday)
Mulligan aggressively for Force of Will — a 5-card hand with FoW beats a 7 without it. T1 Thoughtseize their combo piece or protection (Veil of Summer). Deploy a clock ASAP — every turn they don't combo is a turn you deal damage. Hold Daze mana open T1-3. Post-board: Flusterstorm and Mindbreak Trap beat Veil (neither blue nor black).
C vs Reanimator
FoW the Reanimate/Exhume, not the discard spell. Thoughtseize their Griselbrand or Archon. If they resolve a fatty, race with Murktide (flying ignores ground blockers). Post-board: Nihil Spellbomb is your best card — exile their entire graveyard. FoN counters Reanimate for free on their turn.
A vs Show and Tell
Counter Show and Tell itself — not cantrips. Thoughtseize their Emrakul or Omniscience. Deploy threats fast — Show needs time to assemble. If they resolve Show, you put in your best creature (Murktide or Kaito). Post-board: FoN gives you 5 free counters for their key spell.
B vs Dimir Mirror (all variants)
Win the Bowmasters war — whoever resolves theirs first controls the draw step. Murktide is the trump card (flies over all ground creatures). Wasteland their Underground Sea to cut colors. Protect your threats with Daze. In the Barrowgoyf variant, avoid trading into deathtouch — use removal instead.
B vs DnT / Boros (Vial decks)
FoW their Aether Vial T1 — this is the most important counter of the entire match. Push Thalia immediately (her tax cripples your cantrips). Fly over with Murktide (they have no flyers). Wasteland their Karakas and Rishadan Port. Don't walk into Eidolon — sequence your 1-mana spells before CMC 2+ spells.
B vs UWx Control
Resolve Murktide or Kaito as early as possible — UWx struggles with hexproof and huge flyers. Wasteland their dual lands to cut them off white mana. Play around Terminus — don't commit 3+ creatures to the board at once. Thoughtseize their Monastery Mentor before it generates tokens.
A vs Prison (Artifacts)
FoW Chalice on 1 — it blocks your entire 1-mana package (Brainstorm, Ponder, Push, Tamiyo, Daze). If Trinisphere resolves, you can't FoW for free anymore (alternate costs still pay minimum 3). Deploy creatures T1-2 before locks come down. Post-board: FoN gives more free answers to their artifacts.
B vs Painter
FoW the Painter's Servant — it enables an instant kill with Grindstone (mills your entire library since all cards share a color). Watch for Blood Moon — it turns your Underground Seas into Mountains (only produce red). Push the Grindstone before they activate it. Their Pyroblast counters your blue spells — play around it.
A vs Lands
Free win. Wasteland their key lands (Tabernacle, Dark Depths). They have no creatures until Marit Lage — attack freely for 10+ turns. FoW their Crop Rotation. If they make a 20/20 Marit Lage, Karakas bounces it (it's legendary). Push is dead — board it out for FoN.
A vs Eldrazi / Mardu / UR Aggro
Counter their key T1-2 play (Chalice, Grief, Ragavan). Deploy Nethergoyf as a brick wall — it blocks everything and grows. Murktide flies over their ground creatures for the finish. Wasteland their nonbasic lands to slow development. Fatal Push handles their cheap threats efficiently.
B vs Mono Black
Race Dauthi Voidwalker (shadow — can't block or be blocked). Wasteland their Swamps to cut mana for Braids and Grief. Bowmasters pings on their draw step. Don't let Hymn to Tourach resolve — FoW it if you can, it's devastating 2-for-1 disruption.

Key Plays & Sequencing

Mulligan Rules

KEEP: 2 lands + 1 threat + 1 disruption (FoW/Daze/TS). This is the bread-and-butter hand.
KEEP: 1 land + fetch + Daze + threat. Daze returns the land; you still have mana T2.
MULLIGAN: 0-1 land with no cantrip. All-interaction no-threat hands. No blue source hands.
vs COMBO: Mulligan aggressively for Force of Will. A 5-card hand with FoW beats a 7 without it.
vs AGGRO: Keep removal-heavy hands. Push + Goyf + 2 lands is a keep even without FoW.

Turn 1 Priority Order

T1 Thoughtseize — Strip their best card before they untap. Best T1 play in most matchups.
T1 Fetch + Pass — Hold Daze mana, represent interaction. Best vs combo/Vial decks.
T1 Tamiyo — Only with FoW backup. She dies to Bolt, Push, and Bowmasters pings.
NEVER T1 Brainstorm — No shuffle effect available = the card is wasted. Wait for a fetch.

Critical Sequencing

Brainstorm → Fetch — Put 2 bad cards back on top, then shuffle them away with the fetch. This is the most important 2-card combo in Legacy.
FoW their T1 play — Counter Aether Vial, Chalice of the Void, Dark Ritual, Show and Tell. These are the cards that win games before you untap.
Flash Bowmasters on draw step — Cast on their upkeep/draw for immediate ping + Orc Army token. Every subsequent draw triggers more pings.
Wasteland before they fetch — Destroy their dual land before they can crack a fetch for a replacement. Timing matters.
Daze is best T1-T3 — Opponent is mana-constrained and can't pay {1}. After T4, they'll pay through it.
Kaito survives everything — Hexproof dodges STP, Terminus tucks (but Kaito comes back), Push needs revolt for CMC 3. The stickiest threat in the deck.

Combat Tips

Murktide flies over everything — the finisher vs creature decks (DnT, Boros, Eldrazi).
Nethergoyf grows as the game goes on. Typically 3/4 by T3, 4/5 by T5. Your most reliable attacker.
Never attack with Bowmasters into a board where it would die — the draw-step ping value is worth more than 1 combat damage.
Tamiyo flips after drawing your 3rd card in a turn. Brainstorm alone triggers this (draw 3).
